About Us

Welcome to tarkarighar, your go-to destination for fresh and organic vegetables. Our journey began in 2023 with a vision to provide communities with access to healthy and locally sourced produce.

Our Mission

At FreshVege Market, we are committed to promoting a sustainable and healthy lifestyle. We believe in supporting local farmers, reducing our environmental impact, and delivering the highest quality vegetables to your table.

Our History

Tarkarighar has grown into a leading provider of fresh and organic vegetables. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, working closely with suppliers to ensure a diverse and high-quality product selection.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our success. Join us on our journey towards a healthier and more sustainable future.
